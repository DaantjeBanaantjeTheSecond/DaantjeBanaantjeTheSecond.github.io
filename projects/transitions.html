Transitions
Based on the work “Return to Square”, I was fascinated by the transition between the shape of a square to a silhouette, back to a square. I experimented with a lot of different ways of morphing from one shape to another.

Eventually I came across the movement of a double pendulum. These pendulums can give an extremely different movement with only a slight adjustment in starting position. This made for a great base to use my transition code to.

After a few iterations it ended up in a digital structure that keeps on evolving: growing, schrinking, exploding and collapsing endlessly. Continuously morphing from one shape to the next.
