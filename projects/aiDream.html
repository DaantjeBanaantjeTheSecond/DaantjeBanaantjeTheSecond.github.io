AI Dream
What would an artificial intelligence dream of? In this game you can explore the randomly generated worlds an artificial intelligence might make by dreaming. Just like how a human dreams: firing random neurons, this AI also makes worlds by randomly picking everything: the earth, sky, sun, lights, objects, sound and time.

You can explore these dreams, but only for a random amount of time. After this time, a new world gets generated, and the old one is lost forever. Just like our dreams, quickly fading until completely forgotten.
