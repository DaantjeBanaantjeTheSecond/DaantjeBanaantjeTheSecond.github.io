dark mode
Something that looks like a Cube
My first sound composition. Made from custom made instruments. The recordings of these instruments were edited and composed in Ableton.

The visuals were made with processing. I made a few scenarios that started playing at certain times in the song. All of the scenarios are affected by the amplitude of the song.

The original work was shown on a prism, creating a 3D hologram.
