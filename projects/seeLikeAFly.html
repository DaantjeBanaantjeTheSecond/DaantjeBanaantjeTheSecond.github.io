See like a fly
See like a fly is a work that started from my fascination with the eyes of flies. I wanted to create an experience where I could be able to see just like a fly. With 100â€™s of little eyes all in slightly different angles from each other.

To create this experience I made a raytraced VR game, these rays start in a standard 90 degree angle, just like humans. Then, slowly, the individual angles of the vision rays are edited. Creating new ways of seeing: different fields of view, endless perspective, multiple eyes, exponential and 360 views.

You can download the game on github.
