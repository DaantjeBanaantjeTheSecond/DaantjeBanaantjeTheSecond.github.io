Interference
Interference is a project about data transfer. A laser sends information to a photosensitive diode. This diode translates the received data and displays it on a screen. By blocking the laser, the data stream stops, glitching the image.

This was an interactive installation at the entrance of our end of the year exposition. Visitors had to walk through the laser to enter the exposition. Every time a visitor would walk through the laser the image would start glitching more and more. Resulting in live glitch art.
