Internet Cafe
Everybody has his own personal internet experience. Everybody visits different websites and uses different pc’s.

Big corporations like Google, Facebook, Reddit, Twitter, etc. like to keep this as an illusion with their websites. Personal walls, Subscriptions and video’s for every individual user.

All these personal places on the internet are still kind of the same, because of the way these big corporations set up their services.

In this work I try to show you your own personal place on the internet. By musically visualizing the different sites you use and encounter on the internet. In the installation, all the pc’s are together, so that everybody can get a little insight into other users and their corner of the web.

Together, by just browsing your own web, you can make soundscapes together.

The program is a chrome plugin. You can download it here or check it out on only this page and the home page by pressing the buttons below.
